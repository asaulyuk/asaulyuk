## Selected Work

### Customer Satisfaction Analysis (~78K Reviews)
Analysis of a large-scale European hotel review dataset to identify operational and behavioral drivers of satisfaction.

- Cleanliness shows the strongest continuous relationship with satisfaction (r = 0.296)
- Booking channel and travel type create large structural differences (~19-point gaps)
- Demographics have minimal explanatory power

**Implication:** Customer experience is driven more by service quality and behavioral context than by demographic segmentation.

---

### Advertising Perception Analysis (Gen Y)
Quantitative study (n=266) analyzing drivers of advertising attitudes using regression and hypothesis testing.

- Belief systems (e.g., trust, informativeness) significantly predict attitudes
- Demographics such as gender and income are not meaningful drivers
- Education level correlates with increased skepticism

**Implication:** Effective targeting requires segmentation based on beliefs rather than demographics, with emphasis on credibility and transparency in messaging.
